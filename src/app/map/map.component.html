The Route

The route is one lap and starts and finishes in Wells Market Place, Somerset. The route is undulating (with a few small hills) and multi-terrain with a bias towards road. You will be guaranteed breathtaking views over the Somerset moors and Glastonbury Tor, finishing by the beautiful Wells Cathedral.

Start time: 11am

Cut off time: 5pm (...but we don't think it will take you that long)

This is an UNTIMED RUN, but the roads will be closed and there will be stewards to support you until the last runner goes past.
